Macro Photography Scuba Club

Our Goal

A Scuba diving group focused on Macro Photography.

We are based in Bali, Indonesia and love to dive and find beautiful macro critters.

Such critters can include:

  • Nudibranch
  • Crabs
  • Shrimp
  • Others

Our Club

The Macro Photography Scuba Club was established in 2019 in Bali, Indonesia and has approximately 150 members.

The Scuba Club gets tries to get together every 3rd Saturday of the month.

There is a monthly photograph competition. Winners get to have their winning submission set in our group website until the next meeting.
